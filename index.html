The Legend of Zelda games are made by Nintendo exclusivly for Nintendo consoles.

The main character in most of the Legend of Zelda games is Link.

The first game, called "the Legend of Zelda" was released on Februari 21, 1986. So that means the franchise is a little over 39 years old as of 2025.
